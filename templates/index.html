This site is still in beta, but we would love your support!
We are still in beta testing, but we would love your support. Feel free to reach out via our support form to request features, report a bug and just any comments in general.

Here at CollectorsBoard, we are out to provide a free and open tool to track, value and share your collectables. Want to show people your full collection quickly or get the total value of your entire collection? Try CollectorsBoard.live out!

A few things we currently offer!
  • Create item posts/entrys
  • Join and make posts within a category specific community.
  • Adding your items and other users items to your customizable collections
  • Comment on, share, save items/collections
  • Create entrys/posts with current market value (beta).
  • Add tags such as where and when an item was made, a photo and the official name along with general details
  • Custom feed built for you!
  • Open Source code and full disclosure of what partners we work with!
Features in development:
  • Market Place
  • Live auctions
  • Image galleries for each item
  • Updating prices of items/collections every few hours!
  • Instant Messaging
  • - Admins for communities(please reach out via our support form to claim your spot for a community)
